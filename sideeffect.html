What side effects can be experienced due to desflurane?

Along with the necessary effects, the drug may cause some undesirable effects. Although not all of these side effects can occur, if they occur they may need medical attention. Some side effects may occur that usually do not require medical attention. These side effects may be lost during treatment as your body adjusts to the medication. Also, your doctor may be able to tell you about ways to prevent or reduce some of the side effects. What to know before using desflurane? When deciding to use certain medications, consider the risks and benefits first. This is a decision you and your doctor must make. For desflurane, note the following: Allergy Let your doctor know if you have an unusual or allergic reaction to this drug or other medication. Tell your doctor as well if you have other types of allergies such as food, dyes, preservatives, or animal allergies. For non-prescription products, read the label on the packaging carefully. Children Desflurane has been tested in children. Not used to initiate anesthesia in children who are awake for causing irritation and other undesirable effects. However, when used to continue general anesthesia that has started with other anesthesia, desflurane does not cause different side effects or problems in children than it does in adults. Elderly Desflurane has been tested and has not been proven to cause different side effects or problems in the elderly from young adults. However, the elderly usually require a small amount of dope from a younger person. The doctor will take into account your age when deciding the right amount of desflurane for you.
