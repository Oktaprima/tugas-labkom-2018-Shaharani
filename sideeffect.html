What side effects can be experienced due to desflurane?

Along with the necessary effects, the drug may cause some undesirable effects. Although not all of these side effects can occur, if they occur they may need medical attention. Some side effects may occur that usually do not require medical attention. These side effects may be lost during treatment as your body adjusts to the medication. Also, your doctor may be able to tell you about ways to prevent or reduce some of the side effects. Check with your doctor if any of the following side effects continue to interfere or if you have any questions:

A common occurrence

  • cough
  • nausea or vomiting
  • Rarely happening
  • dizzy
  • headache
  • irritation or red eyes
  • nervous and restless
  • sore throat
    • Not everyone has the following side effects. There may be some side effects not mentioned above. If you have concerns about certain side effects, consult your doctor or pharmacist.

    What health conditions can interact with desflurane?

    Any other health problems in your body can affect the use of this drug. Tell your doctor if you have other health problems especially: diseases that can cause muscle weakness, such as periodic familial palsy, muscular dystrophy, myasthenia gravis, or Eaton-Lambert syndrome heart disease or blood vessels-the possibility of side effects may increase, but serious problems can be prevented if the doctor knows of this condition before giving you an anesthetic malignant hyperthermia, during or shortly after receiving an anesthetic.     What should I do if I miss a dose?

    If you forget a dose of this medicine, drink as soon as possible. But when it comes to the next dose, skip the missed dose and return to the usual dose schedule. Do not double dose.
