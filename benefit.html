Benefit

Desflurane is used to cause total anesthesia (loss of consciousness) before and during surgery. This medicine is inhaled (inhalation). Although desflurane may be used alone, it is also often used in combination with other anesthetic combinations. This helps to produce more effective anesthesia in some patients. Use of this drug should be supervised by a doctor, if any questions about this drug please consulted by an expert physician.

Because desflurane is a common anesis, the administration of this drug should be under direct supervision by a trained medical doctor. If you receive this (anesthetic) medication during surgery, your doctor will administer the medication and continue to monitor your progress.

These drugs are best kept at room temperature, away from direct light and damp places. Do not keep it in the bathroom. Do not freeze. Other brands of these drugs may have different storage rules. Pay attention to the storage instructions on the product packaging or ask your pharmacist. Keep all medicines out of reach of children and pets. Do not flush medicines into the toilet or into the sewer unless instructed. Remove this product when its validity period has expired or when it is no longer needed. Consult a local pharmacist or waste disposal company about how to safely dispose of your product.
